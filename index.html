Tempo Goal Tracker

Tempo Goal Tracker is an app that I've designed and developed on my own.
It is a highly customizable goal and habit tracker app. Tempo shows you detailed charts about the way you spend your time and awards you with badges for each milestone you complete in the app while progressing towards your goal.

Technologies

Swift, Realm, Charts, In-App Purchases, Firebase Authentication and Database, Facebook ads integration, Local Notifications, Watch Kit, Watch Connectivity, Sign in with Apple, Google, Facebook

Chrips

Chirps is a very simple app that lets you schedule small notifications that recur with the specified frequency. I built this app because I needed to schedule a reminder notifying me every 20 minutes and the Reminders app that comes with the iPhone lacks this functionality.

Technologies

Swift, Local Notifications

Biz App

Biz App is the first indie iOS app that I've designed and developed.
My goal with Biz was to offer an alternative to paper business cards, a more modern and environmentally friendly way of exchanging contact information.
You create a digital business card stored in a QR code which you can share with other users of the app.
Biz uses machine learning to also read paper business cards and store them in the app.

Technologies

Swift, Realm, Core ML, Core Location, AVFoundation, RNCryptor, In-App Purchases, Firebase Authentication and Database, Facebook ads integration, Local Notifications, Watch Kit, Watch Connectivity, Sign in with Apple, Google, Facebook

Dates

Dates is a small Swift library containing useful Date extensions and functions

I created these Date extensions during my daily work as a developer. They proved to be very useful for myself in manipulating dates in Swift and presenting them to the user.
The library is under continuous development and I push updates as I create more useful extensions.
